Our Story

Who We Are

Founded by former senior bankers who decided to put client interests first. No quotas. No politics. Just expertise and straight talk.

100+ Years of Banking Experience

Our founders spent decades inside India's leading banks—making credit decisions, structuring complex deals, and navigating the unspoken rules of institutional finance. We've approved loans. We've denied them. And we know exactly what banks look for.

In 2020, we decided to flip the table. Instead of working for the bank, we'd work for the client. That simple shift changed everything.

Today, EBCG is the bridge between ambitious Indians and the capital they need to grow. We're not a bank. We're not a broker. We're the people who've sat on both sides of the table, and now we sit on yours.
