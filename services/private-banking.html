Private Banking Solutions in India

Exclusive lending and wealth structuring for high-net-worth individuals and family offices — guided by former private bankers.

Private Banking Beyond Standard Lending

India's wealth creation over the past two decades has produced a growing cohort of high-net-worth individuals (HNIs) and ultra-high-net-worth families whose financial needs far exceed the capabilities of retail banking products. Private banking in India — offered by institutions like HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak, and dedicated wealth management firms — provides sophisticated lending, investment, and structuring solutions. Yet gaining access to the best private banking products requires more than meeting minimum balance thresholds.

Ex Banker Consulting Group (EBCG) was founded by professionals who built and managed private banking divisions at India's leading financial institutions. We understand the internal criteria that determine which clients receive preferential pricing, which collateral structures unlock higher advance rates, and how family wealth can be structured across generations for optimal tax efficiency and lending access. Our private banking consulting service is designed for discerning clients who expect bespoke solutions, not catalogue products.

Private Banking Services We Advise On

Asset-Backed Lending

HNIs frequently hold significant wealth in illiquid forms — real estate portfolios, unlisted equity, art collections, and business interests. Asset-backed lending allows you to unlock liquidity without selling core holdings. EBCG structures facilities against residential and commercial property, listed and unlisted securities, fixed income portfolios, and insurance policies. We negotiate advance rates and pricing that reflect the true quality of your collateral, not conservative standard grids.

Portfolio Leverage & Investment-Backed Loans

For clients with substantial equity and mutual fund portfolios, Lombard-style lending provides cost-effective liquidity for investment opportunities, business capital calls, or lifestyle needs. We identify lenders offering competitive loan-to-value ratios on diversified portfolios, with flexible drawdown and repayment terms. Our advisors model the impact of market volatility on margin requirements to ensure facilities remain sustainable through market cycles.

Luxury Residential & Second Home Financing

Premium residential properties — penthouses, villas, farmhouses, and international second homes — often exceed standard home loan ticket sizes and fall outside conventional underwriting frameworks. EBCG facilitates jumbo home loans and cross-border property financing through private banking channels that evaluate the complete client relationship rather than isolated transaction metrics.

Business & Promoter Funding

Many HNI clients are business promoters whose personal and corporate finances are intertwined. Private banking lenders offer promoter funding, share pledge facilities, and structured debt that bridges personal and business capital needs. We present integrated financial profiles that demonstrate overall credit strength, enabling facilities that retail business banking channels would decline.

Family Office & Succession Structuring

Wealth transition across generations requires careful planning of ownership structures, trust arrangements, and lending facilities that accommodate changing family dynamics. EBCG works alongside your legal and tax advisors to ensure financing structures align with succession plans, avoiding disruptions to credit facilities during estate transitions.

Who We Serve in Private Banking

  • HNIs and UHNIs with net worth exceeding ₹5 crore seeking sophisticated lending
  • Business families managing wealth across operating companies and personal holdings
  • Professionals — senior executives, doctors, lawyers — with high income and complex asset bases
  • NRIs and returning Indians restructuring wealth upon repatriation
  • Family offices seeking consolidated lending across multiple entities and asset classes
  • Entrepreneurs post-exit or post-IPO requiring liquidity management

The Discretion Imperative

Private banking clients rightly demand the highest levels of confidentiality. EBCG operates as a boutique advisory — not a volume-driven DSA. Client information is protected under strict confidentiality protocols. Meetings are arranged at your convenience, in person or via secure video. We never share client details with lenders until you authorise a specific application, and we present your profile to a carefully selected shortlist rather than broadcasting enquiries across the market.

This discretion extends to pricing negotiations. Private banking rates are inherently negotiable, influenced by total relationship value, collateral quality, and duration of engagement. Our former bankers negotiate from a position of credibility — credit managers take our recommendations seriously because they know we present thoroughly vetted, high-quality clients.

How EBCG Private Banking Consulting Works

  1. Confidential discovery: Understanding your complete financial picture, objectives, and constraints
  2. Solution design: Identifying optimal products and lenders for your specific profile
  3. Relationship introduction: Connecting you with senior private banking teams — not junior relationship managers
  4. Term negotiation: Advocating for preferential pricing, advance rates, and facility flexibility
  5. Ongoing advisory: Annual reviews, facility renewals, and proactive restructuring as needs evolve

Why HNIs Choose EBCG Over Direct Private Banking

Approaching a private bank directly means starting at the bottom of a relationship hierarchy, interacting with RM teams who rotate frequently and lack authority to customise terms. You may receive standard product offerings without awareness of alternatives available at competing institutions or within different divisions of the same bank.

EBCG provides an independent, insider perspective. We maintain relationships across multiple private banking platforms and can objectively recommend the institution best suited to your profile — without the conflict of interest inherent in a bank employee recommending only their own products. When we introduce you to a private banking team, you arrive as a pre-qualified, well-presented client — commanding immediate attention and preferential treatment.

Integrated Wealth & Lending Strategy

Private banking is most effective when lending decisions are integrated with overall wealth strategy. Borrowing against a portfolio to fund a real estate acquisition, using property equity to invest in business expansion, or structuring cross-collateralised facilities across asset classes — these decisions require holistic analysis that siloed product sales cannot provide.

EBCG's advisors bring decades of experience in both credit and wealth management. We help you answer the fundamental question: not just whether you can borrow, but whether you should — and at what structure, from which institution, and with what impact on your broader financial position.

For high-net-worth individuals and families who expect their financial advisors to match their own standards of excellence, EBCG's private banking consulting delivers the insider access, bespoke structuring, and absolute discretion that this clientele demands.
