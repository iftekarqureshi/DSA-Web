Home Loan Consulting in India

Former senior bankers securing the best home loan rates and fastest approvals from 25+ partner banks.

Why Home Loan Consulting Matters in India

India's home loan market is vast and complex. With over 50 banks and NBFCs offering housing finance, interest rates that vary by 100–200 basis points for the same borrower profile, and documentation requirements that differ significantly between lenders, choosing the right home loan is far from straightforward. A difference of just 0.25% in interest rate on a ₹1 crore loan translates to over ₹5 lakh in additional interest over a 20-year tenure.

At Ex Banker Consulting Group (EBCG), our home loan consulting service is built on insider knowledge. Our advisors are former senior bankers who spent years approving — and declining — home loan applications. We know exactly what underwriters look for, which lenders are most competitive for specific profiles, and how to structure applications for the highest approval probability at the lowest cost.

Who We Serve

Our home loan consulting practice serves a diverse clientele across India:

  • First-time homebuyers navigating the property purchase and loan process for the first time
  • Existing homeowners seeking balance transfers to reduce interest costs or access top-up loans
  • NRIs and PIOs investing in Indian residential property from abroad
  • Self-employed professionals whose income documentation requires specialised presentation
  • High-net-worth individuals seeking jumbo home loans above standard ticket sizes
  • Property investors financing second homes or rental properties

Our Home Loan Consulting Services

Rate Comparison & Lender Selection

We maintain active relationships with 25+ partner banks and NBFCs including HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, Bajaj Housing Finance, and LIC Housing Finance. Rather than presenting a generic rate comparison, we match your specific profile — credit score, income type, property location, loan amount — to the lenders most likely to offer competitive terms. This targeted approach consistently delivers rates 20–40 basis points below what borrowers find through direct bank visits or online aggregators.

Application Preparation & Submission

Documentation errors are the leading cause of home loan delays and rejections in India. Our team prepares a complete, bank-ready dossier tailored to your chosen lender's specific requirements. We handle income verification letters, property document review, CIBIL analysis, and form completion — eliminating the back-and-forth that typically adds weeks to the process.

Balance Transfer Advisory

If you are paying a home loan at rates above current market levels, a balance transfer can save lakhs over your remaining tenure. We analyse your existing loan terms, calculate break-even points accounting for processing fees and prepayment penalties, and execute transfers to lenders offering genuinely better economics — not merely marginally lower advertised rates.

NRI Home Loan Expertise

Non-resident Indians face unique challenges: power of attorney requirements, foreign income documentation, FEMA compliance, and lender policies that vary widely. EBCG has successfully facilitated NRI home loans for clients in the UAE, USA, UK, Singapore, and Australia — managing the entire process remotely with dedicated coordination between overseas borrowers and Indian lenders.

Pre-Approval & Property Search Support

Knowing your exact borrowing capacity before property hunting prevents wasted effort and negotiation disadvantage. We secure in-principle approvals that strengthen your position with sellers and developers, particularly in competitive markets like Mumbai, Bangalore, and Delhi NCR.

Current Market Context

India's home loan market continues to grow, driven by urbanisation, government incentives under PMAY, and sustained demand in tier-1 and tier-2 cities. Repo rate movements by the RBI directly impact floating home loan rates, making timing and lender selection critical. EBCG monitors rate movements daily and proactively advises existing clients on balance transfer opportunities when market conditions shift favourably.

Regulatory changes — from RERA compliance requirements to updated LTV norms for affordable housing — also affect loan eligibility and terms. Our advisors stay current on every policy shift so your application reflects the latest guidelines, avoiding delays caused by outdated documentation or incorrect product selection.
